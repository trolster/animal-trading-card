Snow Leopard

In all of history there are only two records of a Snow Leopard attacking a human being.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Habitat: Alpine and subalpine zones
  • Average Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Weight: Between 27 and 55 Kg

Snow leopards are slightly smaller than the other big cats. They have a relatively short body, measuring in length from the head to the base of the tail 75 to 150 cm. However, the tail is quite long, at 80 to 100 cm. They are stocky and short-legged big cats, standing about 60 cm at the shoulder1.
